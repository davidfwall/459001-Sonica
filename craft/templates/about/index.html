At Sonica, we have the highest expectations of ourselves and our work, and complete confidence in our own ability to meet those expectations. That confidence is built on a wealth of expertise in every element of office fit-out from construction to site logistics to tech install. That confidence is built on clear communication, integrity and strong professional relationships. That confidence is built on the knowledge that by pulling in the same direction, our strengths are not just added together, but multiplied. That confidence is built on the experience of 150 completed fit-out projects – and counting.

We take pride and satisfaction in doing things the right way and enjoy facing every challenge. That is what work means to Sonica.

Our Approach

1. Discovery

A great office fit-out begins by getting to know you and your unique needs. We believe that by listening to you and asking the right questions we can form an understanding of what your individual project requires.

2. Planning

When we receive your brief, our first order of business is to assemble an in-house team for your project. We draw on our extensive experience to put together the right staff to handle the individual complexities of your fit-out, before formulating an execution plan and setting delivery targets in stone.

3. Costing

We have an extensive roster of sub-contract partners from whom we assemble the right team for the parameters of your job. Sonica’s Surveying Team then analyses the costings provided by our partners to produce a detailed budget recommendation, which we will present to you to review and sign-off.

The whole process is transparent and allows you to retain complete control of expenditure.

4. Construction

Before any trade works begin on your site, we organise a ramp-up period. This helps us ensure that all our partners begin your project with clarity of purpose, and helps us maintain our impeccable safety record.

Once works begin, our Site Operations Team will provide you with comprehensive weekly reports detailing construction progress and cumulative hours that completed accident-free on your site. Completing your project on-time, on-budget, and accident-free is our only objective.

5. Hand-Over

At Sonica, when we handover your project it means it is 100% finished: no snag lists, no late arrivals. We provide pre-handover training for new installation features for you and your staff, and we’re always happy to help you bed into your new work space and deal with any queries you might have. We believe that diligent organisation is the key to meeting schedules and mitigating against anything getting left behind.

We take pride in making your project our best one yet.

Our Team

From the boardroom to the job site, every member of the Sonica team is driven by the same motivations: to provide a service that goes beyond our clients expectations, and to always strive to improve what we offer.

We believe that by creating an environment founded on encouragement and teamwork, we allow our people to do their best work. That’s something that satisfies both our clients and ourselves.
